Q: For each of the following items of data, specify which one of the types `char`, `short`, `int`, or `long` is the smallest one guaranteed to be large enough to store the item.

(a) Days in a month
(b) Days in a year
(c) Minutes in a day
(d) Seconds in a day

A:

(a) char
(b) short
(c) short
(d) long
